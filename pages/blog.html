Original vs Copy Mobile Parts – Kon Sa Behtar Hai?

Agar aap mobile repair karwa rahe hain to sab se pehle yeh dekhna zaroori hai ke original part lag raha hai ya copy. Original parts zyada durable hote hain aur mobile ki performance bhi behtar rehti hai. Copy parts saste hote hain lekin unki life aur quality kam hoti hai.

Jacobabad jese garm ilaqe me low-quality battery ya display jaldi kharab ho sakti hai. Isi liye hamesha trusted technician se repair karwayen aur original ya high-quality compatible parts hi choose karein.
